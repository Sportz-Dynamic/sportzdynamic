About Us

Sportz Dynamic Enterprise

Established since 2012, we are a sports equipments retailer that has been operating throughout Subang Jaya and Petalling Jaya for more than 10 years. Our primary business includes

  • Sale of sports equipments for a variety of sports.
  • Badminton, Tennis, & Squash Racket Striging Services.
  • Broken Badminton Racket Repair services.
  • Shirt Printing Services.

Throughout the years, we have continued to grow and expand our business across Klang Valley, and now have 3 retail outlets in Putra Heights (Subang Jaya), SS 15 (Subang Jaya), and Sunway PJS (Petalling Jaya).

Services

Sporting Goods Retail

We sell sporting goods for a variety of sports such as Badminton, Squash, Tennis, Basketball, Volleyball, Football, Futsal, Table tennis, etc.

Badminton, Tennis, & Squash Racket Stringing

We provide stringing services for Badminton, Squash, and Tennis rackets.

Badminton Racket Repair

We can repair your broken badminton rackets if it is still intact.

Shirt Printing

We provide shirt printing services in bulk, such as printing team jerseys.

Frequently Asked Questions


  • 01 Do you offer badminton stringing services?

    Yes, we provide stringing services for Badminton, Squash, and Tennis.

  • 02 What type of strings do you have?

    We offer a wide selection of Badminton strings, but we only offer some selection of Tennis and Squash strings. We can also string your rackets if you provide us with your own string.

  • 03 How long will it take to do Badminton, Tennis, or Squash stringing?

    For Badminton rackets, the duration needed to complete the stringing services will depend on the current queue of rackets needed to be strung, if there are no other rackets in queue, we will be able to complete it within the hour. However, Tennis and Squash stringing services will take around 1-2 days.
